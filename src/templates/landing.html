AI-assisted flight planning, built for pilots.

AIVFR helps you plan UK VFR flights with AI-backed insight; surfacing considerations, checking your work, and helping you think through decisions. Not to replace your judgement, but to help you make it.

Oh, and it's free and open source!

Features

So... What does it actually do?

I've tried to develop AIVFR to be easily integrated into your existing workflow.

AI Flight Planning Assistant

AI can generate full flight plans, full flight breifs considering weather, weight and balance, and more. It can also help you find alternate solutions to problems such as overloading.

Built to work with your tools

Official Documentation

A passion project made from scratch

The very first public version of this tool v1.0.0-beta was made as part of the 2026 OCR Computer Science A-level Non-Exam Assessment, and was completely from scratch. Its the most ambitious project I had ever done, and was fundamental in my journey of learning web-development and coding in general! It received an A* with full marks!

If you'd like to see the full documentation of the initial development of this webapp, or just want an exemplar to follow for your own NEA, you can download it here. I guarantee you'll find it interesting!
